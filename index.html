Ingredients

  • 3 sticks Butter
  • 3 cups Sugar
  • 5 whole Eggs
  • 1 teaspoon Butter Flavoring
  • 2 teaspoons Lemon Flavoring
  • 3 cups All-purpose Flour
  • 1 cup Sprite, 7-UP, Or Sierra Mist

Pound Cake Recipe

Description

Pound cake refers to a type of cake traditionally made with a pound of each of four ingredients: flour, butter, eggs, and sugar. However, any cake made with a 1:1:1:1 ratio, by weight, of flour, butter, eggs, and sugar may also be called a pound cake, as it yields the same results.

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees.
  2. Cream butter.
  3. Add sugar, 1 cup at a time, mixing after each addition.
  4. Add eggs, 1 at a time, mixing after each addition.
  5. dd butter and lemon extracts and mix well.
  6. Add flour, 1 cup at a time, mixing well after each addition.
  7. Add soft drink, then mix together until combined.
  8. Scrape sides of bowl, then mix briefly.
  9. Pour into a greased Bundt pan and bake for 1 hour to 1 hour 10 minutes, until the cake is no longer jiggly.
  10. Remove cake from oven and invert pan until cake drops out. Slice and chow down!
